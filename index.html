Welcome


Broward Plastic Surgery is a facility dedicated to providing patients with the highest quality medical and personal care in the realms of facial and body improvements. Each aspect of patient care from the first consultation to the last visit receives the same careful consideration. Rainer Sachse, MD, FACS, a board certified Plastic and Reconstructive surgeon leads the institute.

 

Once you've browsed our site, feel free to contact our office directly for more information. We can be reached at 954.202.9898, and our office hours are 9-5, Monday thru Friday.

 

Our hospital affiliations include Holy Cross, Imperial Point Medical Center, Broward General Medical Center, North Broward Medical Center, and the Surgery Center of Ft. Lauderdale.
